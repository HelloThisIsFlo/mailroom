**Structural issue:** `TestRunResetConfirmation` in `test_resetter.py` does not mock `configure_logging`, which globally rebinds structlog's `PrintLoggerFactory` to live `sys.stderr`. After pytest's capsys closes that handle, screener workflow tests fail with `ValueError: I/O operation on closed file`. Fix: add `monkeypatch.setattr(resetter_mod, "configure_logging", lambda level: None)` in `_run_reset_with_mocks()`.
