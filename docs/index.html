Built for peace of mind

Everything you need for effortless email triage

Smart Triage

One label routes an entire sender. All their backlogged emails move to the right destination, and every future email auto-routes — no repeat work.

Contact Intelligence

Automatically creates contacts — person or company — and assigns them to the right group. Display names are extracted and preserved.

Retry Safety

If something fails, the triage label stays put. The next poll picks it up and tries again automatically. Nothing slips through the cracks.

Fully Configurable

Labels, contact groups, polling interval, destinations — all customizable via environment variables. Fits your Fastmail setup, not the other way around.

Built to grow

Mailroom is just getting started. The engine is open source and designed for extensibility.
